# Privacy Policy

**Effective Date:** July 8, 2025

## 1. Information We Collect

When you use Senko-San, we may collect the following information:

### Automatically Collected Information:
- Discord user IDs (numerical identifiers)
- Discord server/guild IDs
- Channel IDs where commands are used
- Timestamps of bot interactions
- Command usage data and statistics

### User-Provided Information:
- Messages sent as commands to the bot
- Any data you voluntarily provide through bot commands
- Server configuration settings

## 2. How We Use Your Information

We use collected information to:
- Provide and maintain the bot's functionality
- Respond to your commands and requests
- Improve the bot's performance and features
- Generate usage statistics and analytics
- Debug issues and provide technical support

## 3. Data Storage and Security

- All data is stored securely on our servers
- We implement reasonable security measures to protect your information
- Data is retained only as long as necessary for the bot's operation
- We do not store message content beyond what's necessary for command processing

## 4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal information with third parties, except:
- When required by law or legal process
- To protect our rights or the safety of users
- With your explicit consent

## 5. Third-Party Services

Senko-San may integrate with third-party services (such as APIs). These services have their own privacy policies, and we encourage you to review them.

## 6. Data Deletion

You can request deletion of your data by:
- Contacting us directly
- Removing the bot from your server
- Using any built-in data deletion commands (if available)

## 7. Children's Privacy

The bot is not intended for users under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

## 8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. We will notify users of significant changes through appropriate channels.

## 9. Contact Information

For questions about this Privacy Policy or to request data deletion, contact: _blazzy on Discord
